MY FUNDAMENTALS

Web Development

I work confidently with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, creating responsive and accessible websites. My focus is on mobile-first design, ensuring smooth user experiences across devices. This foundation supports all my digital projects.

Framework & Performance

With Astro, I build fast and efficient web projects tailored for performance. I complement this with Supabase, connecting frontends to real-time data and dynamic features. Together, they allow me to deliver modern, scalable solutions.

AI & Creative Tools

I explore ChatGPT for brainstorming, problem-solving, and refining ideas. In addition, AI image generation gives me new creative possibilities, expanding beyond traditional methods. Both support faster workflows and fresh perspectives.

Design & Layout

I use Photoshop, Artstudio Pro, and Figma to shape ideas into polished designs. From concept sketches to detailed layouts, I adapt tools to fit the workflow. My aim is always clarity, balance, and visual impact.

Content & Presentation

Through Premiere Pro and Illustrator, I create video and graphic content that communicates effectively. I enjoy combining visuals with storytelling to make ideas stand out. These tools help me bridge creativity with clear messaging.

Collaboration

Using GitHub, I organize projects, track versions, and collaborate efficiently. Clean workflows are important to me, whether solo or in a team. This ensures projects remain structured and easy to build upon.
